FAQ

We accept statutory and private health insurances as well as self-payers.
Please arrive 10 minutes before your appointment. You should bring your medical documents with you. It is worth eating and brushing your teeth beforehand, since it is often recommended not to eat for 2 hours after your treatment.
It is important to every doctor in our practice that your appointment is pleasant and pain-free, which is why we treat every patient with great care and attention. If necessary, we administer an anaesthetic during the appointment.
We undergo regular training and only use proven quality materials and equipment that are suitable for your case. We guarantee that you will receive the best treatment and service at our practice, especially for dental fillings. We grant a 2-year guarantee on all treatments.
If a cost-intensive treatment is pending, we will draw up a treatment and cost plan so that you can coordinate this with your insurance company.
In addition to private transport, you can reach us by the underground line U2 or by the bus lines 116, 610, DIREKT and ERSATZ. Our practice is located opposite the underground station and bus station “Rauhes Haus”.
